## Hard Constraints

- Data involving minors must be minimally collected with guardian consent.
- Learning recommendations must not replace final judgment of teachers or institutions.
- Content recommendations must pass safety and age-appropriateness checks.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Prior to GA, must provide guardian consent, data minimization, content safety, and human review evidence.
